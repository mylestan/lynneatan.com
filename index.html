ExoGear

Solved the issue of numerous lower arm injuries to Paralympic sledge hockey players with a Reinforced Exoskeleton Glove. Glove was made out of spandex and was reinforced with Lexan and 3D printed ABS-PC plastic pieces. The ExoGear was to be worn underneath the regular hockey glove.
